About Gurcharan Singh

A dedicated officer at the intersection of law enforcement and technology, protecting Punjab's digital frontier.

Gurcharan Singh serves as the Information Technology Officer in Punjab Police's Cyber Crime Wing, bringing years of specialized experience in digital forensics, cyber crime investigation, and network security operations. As a key member of the state's digital task force, he bridges the gap between traditional policing and modern technological warfare.

With deep expertise in examining digital devices, recovering deleted data, analyzing network traffic, and tracing cryptocurrency transactions, Gurcharan Singh has been instrumental in solving complex cyber fraud cases, online harassment investigations, and data breach incidents that impact citizens and institutions across Punjab.

His work ensures that digital evidence collected during investigations meets the highest standards of forensic integrity, standing up to judicial scrutiny in courts of law. He also leads training programs that equip field officers with essential cyber awareness and digital evidence handling skills.

01

Incident Reporting & Triage

Cyber crime complaints received through 1930 helpline, online portal, or field units are triaged based on severity — financial fraud, data breach, or threat to critical infrastructure.

02

Device Seizure & Preservation

Digital devices are seized following legal protocols under CrPC & IT Act. Write-blockers ensure no data modification. Cryptographic hash values (SHA-256) are generated immediately for integrity verification.

03

Forensic Extraction & Analysis

Using tools like Cellebrite UFED, FTK Imager, and EnCase, deleted files, communication logs, browsing history, and metadata are extracted and analyzed for investigative leads.

04

Network & IP Tracing

IP address tracing, ISP coordination, DNS log analysis, and VPN/proxy de-anonymization techniques are employed to identify perpetrators and establish the digital trail.

05

Report Generation & Court Presentation

Comprehensive forensic reports with screenshots, hash verifications, and expert opinions are prepared for court presentation under Section 65B of the Indian Evidence Act.

06

Arrest & Prosecution Support

Technical support during raids, assistance in preparing chargesheets with digital evidence annexures, and expert testimony during trial proceedings.

All digital evidence processed under Gurcharan Singh's supervision complies with Section 65B requirements, including proper certification of electronic records, hash-based integrity verification, and documented chain of custody — ensuring maximum admissibility in courts of law across Punjab.

Public Awareness

Cyber Safety Advisories

Prevention is the first line of defense. Gurcharan Singh's team regularly conducts cyber awareness campaigns across schools, colleges, and community centers in Punjab.

Report Cyber Crime

If you are a victim of cyber crime, report immediately:

Helpline: 1930

Website: cybercrime.gov.in

Punjab Cyber Cell: 112

Beware of UPI & Phishing Scams

Never share OTP, PIN, or click on suspicious links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email. Verify caller identity before making any transaction. Punjab Police will never call you asking for banking details.

Secure Your Digital Accounts

Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts. Use strong, unique passwords. Never use public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions. Keep your devices updated with latest security patches.

Protect Children Online

Monitor children's online activity. Educate them about stranger danger online, cyberbullying, and inappropriate content. Use parental control software and report any suspicious interactions immediately.

Think Before You Share

Do not share personal information, Aadhaar, PAN, or bank details on social media. Be cautious of fake job offers, lottery scams, and "friend in distress" requests. Verify before forwarding any information.

Investment & Crypto Scam Alert

Beware of guaranteed-return investment schemes promoted on social media or messaging apps. Unregulated crypto trading platforms promising extraordinary profits are almost always scams. Research thoroughly before investing.
